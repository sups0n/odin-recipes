Lasgna

This is a delicous homemade lasgna from scratch.

Ingredients:

  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  1. Pre cook noodles al dente.
  2. Layer Noodles, both types of cheese, and sauce on however many layers you'd like.
  3. Bake in oven until melted and noodles softened.
  4. Enjoy!!!
