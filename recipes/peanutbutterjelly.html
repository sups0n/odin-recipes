Peanut Butter and Jelly

Our handcrafted, world famous Peanut Butter and Jelly.

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Bread
  • Jelly
  1. Get two pieces of your bread of choice. We recommend soft white bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter onto one piece of bread.
  3. Spread jelly onto the other piece of bread.
  4. Combine the peanut butter side of bread with the jelly side of bread. Enjoy!
